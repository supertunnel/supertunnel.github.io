Welcome. This is Intelligent Tunneling and Engineering Monitoring (ITEM) Research Group at School of Civil Engineering, Guangzhou University. Our research focuses on the development and application of numerical approaches on geotechnical engineering and geology process in nature, such as tunneling (especially undersea tunnel), urban excavation, and smart sensing of underground structures, etc. Currently, we are working on the following topics:

(1) Multi-scale coupling mechanism and intelligent early warning of undersea shield tunneling disasters under complex geological conditions (funded by NSFC, No. 52178385).

(2) The macro-micro mechanism of solid-fluid coupling effect during synchronized grouting of shield tunneling in sandy-cobble strata (funded by NSFC, No. 51808150).

(3) Multiscale modeling for the soil responses during failure process in tunneling (funded by Guangdong-NSF, No. 2018A030313132).

(4) Smart WSN monitoring on the ground responses induced by urban excavation (funded by Guangzhou Science and Technology Programme, No. 202201020171 and No. 202102020617).
